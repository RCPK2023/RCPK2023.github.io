My Homelab

Welcome to a quick peek into my personal homelab — a collection of machines and services duct-taped together with hope and YAML.

  • OPNsense Firewall & WireGuard VPN
  • Technitium DNS + AdGuard Home
  • Active Directory with AD DS, DHCP, and DNS
  • Wazuh SIEM for monitoring logs like a paranoid squirrel
  • Jellyfin — because streaming should be free and flaky
  • Nginx Reverse Proxy, running containerized services

Setup for both self-education and painful troubleshooting sessions at 3AM. Still under constant tinkering.
